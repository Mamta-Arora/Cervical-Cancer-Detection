Symptoms

Menstrual bleeding that is longer and heavier than usual

If Menstrual bleeding during periods often occurs for longer days and is heavy than normal flow then you muct recommend a doctor.

Blood spots or light bleeding between or following periods

Bleeding in between the dates of two periods is a symptom for Cervical Cancer at an advance stage.

Bleeding after intercourse, douching, or a pelvic examination

Bleeding after intercourse even after the first time shows the possibility of Cervical Cancer at an advance stage.
